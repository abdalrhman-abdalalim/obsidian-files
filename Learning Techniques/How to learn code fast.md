
### The 10,000 hour rule 
- You can be master if you practice 10,00 hours.
- The important thing to follow is **Deliberate practice**.
- #### Deliberate practice :
	- ##### **clear , well-defined goals**
		- practice or study what skill exactly are you trying to improve 
		- each session must have a specific goal.
	- ##### **100% focus & attention**
		- you mustn't play podcasts or Youtube video in background.
		- you must go very deep into thought maybe you are thinking about what code you should write next.
	- ##### **occurs outside comfort zone**
		- let us say that you learn some difficult topic the comfort zone is to read articles & watch video , the uncomforted zone is to practice this knowledge in real problem.
